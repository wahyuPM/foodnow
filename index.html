How it's Works

Using FoodNow is as easy as 1..2..3

  • 1Select a restaurant or type of food from the available categories and recommendations that GO-FOOD has personalised only for you.
  • 2Or enter the name of a restaurant or type of food you would like.
  • 3Select the type and quantity of dish(es) you would like to order.
  • 4Enter the delivery address and select the payment method, then click on order
